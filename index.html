Instructions

Click anywhere to insert a box

Right click on a box to enter a value; press escape to cancel

Hover over a box and press X to delete it

Press F on a box to select/unselect it; a line will be drawn between two selections

Press backspace/escape to deselect all

Supported Symbols

+   → Addition

-   → Subtraction

*   → Multiplication

/   → Addition

** → Exponent
